Facility

Our building may be small, but it is still unfamiliar to you. We want you to feel completely comfortable and at home. The information that follows will explain where things are.

Restrooms

Gender specific restrooms are located to the right of the vestibule separating the sanctuary and the fellowship hall.

Class Rooms

Children's and teenage Sunday/Bible study classrooms are located to the right and left of the front entrance.

Fellowship Hall

The fellowship hall is located through the kitchen and down stairs to the basement.
