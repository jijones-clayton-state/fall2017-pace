We are delighted you have taken an interest in Trinity United Methodist Church by visiting with us today. While you are here, we hope that you will take a closer look at us, because we are confident that you will discover a local church body that welcomes you, and place filled with answers to some of the spiritual questions you may. Trinity is known as the small church with a loving heart.

More importantly, you will find that God is here. If you have not already met the Lord, you can come to know Him here. Jesus said, "And this is eternal life, that they know you the only true God, and Jesus Christ whom you have sent.“ (John 17:3 NIV). Saint Augustine said, “Our hearts are restless until they discover rest in God." He was right. If the truth were told; then we were made in the image and likeness of God and are not truly happy until we are at rest in Him. At Trinity United Methodist Church, your aching heart can find rest in God.

It may give you relief to know that you will not be alone. You will also find real people here. You will meet others who have begun life restless and have come to find rest in God. At Trinity United Methodist Church, you will find fellowship and community. We are part of the family of God and would rejoice to have you join us.

In short. you will find us to be a God centered, caring community of individuals who are enthusiastic about having the chance to extend to you the same love, hope and happiness we have received from the Lord. We extend to you a cordial invitation tojoin us this coming Lord’s Day as the family assembles to draw near to God through prayer, thoughtful contemporary songs, traditional hymns, great gospel spirituals and inspiring relevant messages from God’s word. After the worship service, we tend to linger and talk to one another. So, as we seek you out please take a moment to get to know us better.

Yours in Christ,

Pastor Jacqueline Johnson
