About Us:

We are so glad you finally found us! Trinity United Methodist Church is known as the small church with a warm and loving heart. We are a congregation of believers in Christ who aspire each day to live out our faith in the Living God. We invite you to worship the Lord with us. Our friendliness and open acceptance will make you feel welcomed and at home. It is our prayer that once you've experienced our lively worship service you will feel a sense of connection that will shout yes within your heart. Yes, this is is a fellowship to which you can fit in. Yes, it is a place where you can freely worship God. Yes, you are welcome to use your gifts and graces to glorify the name of the Lord. Yes, your ideas will be appreciated. We look forward to seeing you soon!
